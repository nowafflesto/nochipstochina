Common questions

Before you ask

Who is behind this site?
We're a group of private citizens who believe that AI chip export policy is one of the most consequential and under-discussed national security issues of our time. We have no affiliation with any government agency, political party, or corporation.
Are you affiliated with Anthropic or Dario Amodei?
No. We are independent. We quote Dario Amodei's essay because we believe his argument is clear, compelling, and correct — but we have no formal connection to Anthropic.
Is this a partisan issue?
No. Support for chip export controls has come from members of both parties. This is a national security question, not a left-right one. We welcome support from across the political spectrum.
How can I help?
Visit our Take Action page for specific steps you can take right now, including contacting your representatives and sharing this issue with your network. You can also sign up for our email list to stay informed as the policy landscape evolves.
